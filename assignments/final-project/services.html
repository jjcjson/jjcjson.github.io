Our Dedication to Temple Work

At Temple Inn & Suites we care about our ancestors. During your stay, you'll enjoy a setting where history is all around you in the decorations and layout of the main areas. We're dedicated to supporting you in your efforts to find your ancestors as well.

Each Temple Inn & Suites has a fully operational Family History Center. All guests enjoy hands-on help from our professional consultants at no cost. Our staff is specifically trained to help you find "the one." We'll even print your cards for you so you can do the work for your names right after you find them!

Amenities We Offer:

  • Changing rooms for temple patrons
  • Playroom for children and affordable baby-sitting services
  • Family History center with free professional consulting
  • Free hot breakfast every morning
  • Modern exercise/workout room
  • Indoor Pool
  • Free high-speed internet and state-of-the-art TVs in each room
  • Shuttle service to and from the temple
