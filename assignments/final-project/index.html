Part of the foundation for this temple was also made with stones signed by all of the children from the local area.

What to do during your next family temple trip

  1. Plan a Picnic

    2. Bring a blanket, some grub, and enjoy a beautiful picnic near the temple. Great for kids of all ages.

  2. Identify the Plantlife

    3. All of the landscaping on temple grounds is made with plants native to the area around that temple. The plants are also raised in the season they would've normally bloomed in. For example, during certain seasons you can find peppers growing on temple grounds. Have fun as a family learning about the various plants that grow on the temple grounds!

  3. Bring Family Names

    4. Take advantage of our family history consultants and find some ancestors to bring to the temple with you. At our center, we can help every member of the family bring a name to the temple.
